Building Content That Performs

I'm a Content Marketing Specialist and Video Editor with over seven years of experience helping brands grow through strategic storytelling, video production, and digital marketing campaigns.

My work sits at the intersection of creativity, strategy, and technology. I've developed marketing content and growth strategies for educational institutions, media organisations, creative agencies, and technology brands.

From designing content calendars and editing high-retention social media videos to executing full marketing campaigns, I focus on building content that does more than look good — it performs.

Before transitioning fully into digital marketing and content production, I worked in broadcast engineering where I helped design and manage professional broadcast systems. That technical background continues to influence my creative work today, allowing me to approach storytelling with a structured, systems-driven mindset.

Currently, I'm expanding my technical expertise by studying Software Engineering at Miva University.
